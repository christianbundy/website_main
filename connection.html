    CryptoStorm is different from other "privacy services" in that we do not know and do not want to know who you are and have purposefully designed our network to make figuring out who you are nigh impossible.

    I addition, because we use per session transient keys with a twenty-minute rotation, there does not exist a method for us to be forced to turn over encryption keys for past traffic. Most other "privacy services" will hand over their master key to decrypt all past and future traffic along with your contact and billing information, CryptoStorm does not have such a key or information to hand over.
